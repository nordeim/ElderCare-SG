Day Programs

  • Chef-designed meals with dietary accommodations

🧠 Specialised dementia enrichment in a calm studio

Memory Care Mornings

Half-day cognitive therapies focusing on reminiscence, sensory art, and music tailored to early and mid-stage dementia.

  • Alzheimer’s and dementia-certified coaches
  • Small group engagement capped at six per coach
  • Family debrief with care goals every fortnight
Learn more
🎉 Saturday & Sunday relief with curated social outings

Weekend Respite Club

Weekend-only coverage combining escorted excursions, community brunch, and restorative care blocks.

  • Transportation bundled within central districts
  • Curated outings to parks, museums, and hawker tours
  • Priority waitlist for weekday upgrades
Learn more
🌙 Overnight respite with circadian-friendly routines

Night Owl Support

Twilight-to-dawn supervision featuring sleep coaching, medication adherence, and midnight snack service.

  • Sleep hygiene specialists with gerontology focus
  • Dedicated quiet suites and night monitoring tech
  • Optional allied health check-ins at 7am handover

📚 Evening intensives for confident home caregiving

Caregiver Masterclasses

Four-week rotational curriculum covering transfers, behavioural cues, nutrition planning, and caregiver resilience.

  • Taught by interdisciplinary clinical team
  • Workbook and video library with bilingual subtitles
  • Includes respite credits for on-site practice sessions

Our Philosophy

Safety, dignity, and joyful engagement guide every moment

From evidence-backed routines to personalised care plans, we nurture seniors with respect and warmth. Our licensed team collaborates with families to craft experiences that uplift body, mind, and spirit.

  • MOH-certified nurses and therapists onsite daily.
  • Daily reporting and caregiver briefings keep families informed.
  • Inclusive programming accommodating mobility and cognitive needs.
